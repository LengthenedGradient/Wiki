## Autocannon

- A small cannon with a magazine of decent size, these can reasonably harm most light vehicles, and in higher calibers even scare heavier vehicles.

{% include image.html src="weapons/autocannon.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Cannon

- As ancient as it gets, there isn't much to go wrong. You put a shell in and pull the trigger to send death down range.

{% include image.html src="weapons/cannon.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Grenade Launcher

- A small weapon capable of throwing spicy rocks. Not useful for much except killing infantry and annoying armored vehicles.

{% include image.html src="weapons/grenadelauncher.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Heavy Machinegun

- Not highly accurate, but capable of shooting a few more round types than normal machine guns. They take a while to reload and burn through ammo fast, so make sure the gun is pointed first!

{% include image.html src="weapons/heavymachineguns.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Howitzer

- Not as high velocity as cannons, but they can send a decent payload down range. Good for indirect fire. Also have good HEAT performance.

{% include image.html src="weapons/howitzer.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Machinegun

- Not new technology, these are very capable of putting a lot of bullets down range, just don't hope to kill tanks with it any time soon.

{% include image.html src="weapons/machinegun.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Mortar

- Very low muzzle velocity but high payload capacity, these are excellent for indirect fire.

{% include image.html src="weapons/mortar.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Rotary Autocannon

- A small revolving autocannon, capable of putting a lot of bullets in the air very quickly, but overheating very quickly. Very poor accuracy and very heavy for their size.

{% include image.html src="weapons/rotaryautocannon.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Semiautomatic Cannon

- A sizable autocannon that has a 5 round magazine. Payload is quite capable, and can send those 5 rounds out very quickly.

{% include image.html src="weapons/semiautocannon.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Smoke Launcher

- It chucks a little smoke round out, hiding you so you can make a getaway, maybe even alive.

{% include image.html src="weapons/smokelauncher.png" width="25%" %}

---

## Short Cannon

- If you need to skim a little weight, these can work almost as well as normal cannons, if you look past the lower accuracy.

{% include image.html src="weapons/shortcannon.png" width="25%" %}
