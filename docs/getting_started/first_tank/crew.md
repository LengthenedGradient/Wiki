# Crew
Crew affect entities they are linked to based on their "efficiency":
- Drivers affect fuel efficiency
- Gunners affect accuracy
- Loaders affect gun reload speed
- Commanders affect the efficiency of all crew in the vehicle

Not having crew results in debillitating efficiencies (up to 10x worse)

Add crew to the tank with these roles:

{% include image.html src="crew/setup.png" %}

Link the crew like so:
- Drivers to baseplate
- Gunnres to horizontal turret ring
- Loaders to gun
- Commanders should not be linked
